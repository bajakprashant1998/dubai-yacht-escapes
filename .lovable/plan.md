### Services Insert Format
Each service includes:
- Title, slug, description, long_description
- Price (AED) with original_price for discounts
- Duration, rating, review_count
- Highlights (5-6 items)
- Included/excluded lists
- Category assignment
- is_featured flag for popular activities
- Booking type (per_person)
- Cancellation policy
